https://gnems19.itch.io/strapped

You have been inexplicably abducted by an evil corporation, your purpose unknown and your fate uncertain. Trapped and bound with remote explosives threatening your every move, you must summon every ounce of courage to escape their clutches. Will you survive your abduction, or will you succumb to this malevolent force? 

If you like cute retro pixel art games that don't last long, you should check this out!
